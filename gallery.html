Achievements

I'm not a professional photographer - I've never taken any classes and I'm entirely self-taught with both taking photos on my Canon EOS 60D, and in editing in Lightroom, Luminar and Photomatix. That being said - I have some achievements I'm proud of. Here are a few.

  1. Hosted photography competitions on Gurushots with over 136 million participants. [1] [2] [3]
  2. I turned some of my shots into Google Chrome Themes, which have thousands of users [4] [5] [6].
  3. I've been the sole photographer for 3 weddings, 1 military sports event, several galas and balls and GIStandards flew me to Trento, Italy in 2013 to be the photographer for a conference [7] [8] [9]
