Fingerstyle Guitar

I've been playing guitar since I was about 11, mostly fingerstyle acoustic these days. I learn covers and arrangements of video game and film soundtracks, pulling from players like Andy McKee, Beyond The Guitar, and Isac Saleh.

Recordings are on their way to YouTube and TikTok. In the meantime, there's more on music and tech on the blog.
